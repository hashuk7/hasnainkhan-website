Case Study

Najma Masha Innovations — WordPress Rebuild & Ongoing Maintenance

I rebuilt, launched, and continue to maintain the website for Najma Masha Innovations (nminnovations.net), focusing on a clean design refresh, performance, security hardening, and a maintainable WordPress architecture.

Visit Live Site Talk about a similar project

Project Overview

Role: Lead Developer & Site Owner for rebuild and maintenance — timeline: 2023–present. This was a full WordPress rebuild from the ground up with a focus on content clarity, conversion, and long-term maintainability.

Challenges

  • Outdated theme and inconsistent design language across pages.
  • Poor loading performance and unoptimized assets.
  • Maintenance backlog and security concerns from older plugins.

Approach

  • Custom WordPress theme with a clear component structure for easy updates.
  • Design refresh focused on readable typography, clear CTAs, and service-first layouts.
  • Performance tuning: image optimization, critical CSS, and caching strategy.
  • Security hardening and regular maintenance schedule.

Technical Stack & Notes

Stack

  • WordPress (custom theme)
  • Elementor / ACF (where useful for content editors)
  • WPRocket / caching strategy, image optimization
  • Stripe / Payment integration where required

Maintenance

  • Weekly plugin and core checks, monthly performance audit.
  • Security hardening, regular backups, and uptime monitoring.
  • Content updates and design tweaks as requested by the client.

Outcome

The site now presents a consistent brand experience, is easier for the client to edit, and has a maintainable technical foundation. Ongoing maintenance prevents regressions and keeps the site secure and performant.
